Languages

As someone who grew up not knowing english I have grown to appreciate languages and there diversity. In truth I dont remember "learning english" since I was a young child when I became exposed to it. I have come to respect and admire different languages, and so throught my schooling decided to delve into as many as I had access to. As of today I'm still learning as much as I can. I am fluent in spanish and english. I am trying to become proficient in ASL (American sign language). In high school and college I studied French and Japanese. I wish to continue studying for as long as I am on this earth and am now exited to add coding to the list of languages that I am passionate about.
